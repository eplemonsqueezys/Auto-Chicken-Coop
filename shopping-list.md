## Tip: Buy as Packs to Save Money

These items are cheaper in kits — search for these instead of individual pieces:

- **"Arduino starter kit"** — gets you resistors, LEDs, breadboard, jumpers, relays all at once
- **"PCA9685 servo driver"** — usually sold as a module with headers pre-soldered
- **"4-pack relay module 5V"** — you need 3 relays total (fan + 2 lights)
